Alumni Hall of Fame

The Manual Alumni Student Recognition Association (MASRA) is proud to announce the induction of outstanding individuals into the Manual High School Distinguished Alumni and Athletic Alumni Hall of Fame. The induction ceremony will take place on Saturday, June 6, 2026, at 5:30 pm at the Peoria Manual High School Main Gymnasium. Tickets are available for $40 and can be purchased at the MASRA website or by contacting Hosea Washington.
