Larry Hendricks

Street named after former Manual High School Teacher Larry Hendricks who has been an art instructor and renowned coach at Manual High School for the past thirty-four years. During his time he created Manual’s High School’s first afro club. He is also known for his creative side, and was called the “Godfather of Peoria’s talent shows”.
